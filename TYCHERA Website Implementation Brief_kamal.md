## **3\. Company Profile**

### **About Us**

Founded in 2025 by Kamal Alawo ADJAYI, TYCHERA Investments Ltd is a financial engineering and project finance firm focused on infrastructure and public-private partnership projects across Africa. With operational hubs in Luxembourg, Kigali, and Bissau, the firm bridges European capital markets with African growth opportunities through disciplined structuring and long-term investment strategies.

TYCHERA operates across selected sectors, including digital solutions, natural resources, agriculture, healthcare, and infrastructure, with a strong emphasis on sustainable and environmentally responsible projects. Our objective is to deliver projects that generate durable financial returns while creating positive social and environmental outcomes.

### **Vision**

To become a leading African investment and financing firm, recognized for delivering sustainable, high-impact projects that drive economic growth, social development, and environmental stewardship.

### **Mission**

* Structure and finance strategic infrastructure and PPP projects through tailored financial solutions.  
* Promote sustainable, green, and socially responsible investments.  
* Support economic growth, employment, and community development across Africa.  
* Leverage a tri-regional presence to connect investors, governments, and private partners.

### **Core Values**

* **Integrity**: Acting with transparency, ethics, and accountability.  
* **Discipline**: Applying rigorous analysis and long-term thinking to every decision.  
* **Innovation**: Developing practical and forward-looking financial solutions.  
* **Sustainability**: Prioritizing projects with enduring social and environmental value.  
* **Partnership**: Building trusted, long-term relationships with stakeholders.

### **Geographical Presence**

TYCHERA’s strategic hubs provide access to high-potential markets and institutional networks:

* **Luxembourg**: Access to international investors, capital markets, and regulatory expertise, reflecting the country’s role as a leading global financial center.  
* **Kigali**: A regional hub for East Africa, recognized for stability, good governance, and a favorable business environment.  
* **Bissau**: A gateway to West Africa, integrated within UEMOA and ECOWAS, offering access to a region of strong economic and political integration.

This positioning enables cross-border project structuring and efficient capital mobilization.
